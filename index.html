How It Works

Most stock prediction sites use technical indicators and pattern recognition. We don't. Our edge comes from cause-and-effect analysis -- understanding WHY prices move, not just predicting WHEN.

  • Geopolitical events and their downstream effects on specific sectors
  • Earnings catalysts with asymmetric risk/reward setups
  • Macro regime shifts (interest rates, commodity shocks, policy changes)
  • Supply chain disruptions and sector rotation signals
  • News sentiment vs. price action divergences

Every pick includes a full written thesis explaining the cause-and-effect chain. No black-box AI scores. No "buy because the chart says so." Real analysis you can evaluate yourself.
